This article reports on *Japan AI Alignment Symposium 2024* (JSAI Journal, Vol.39 No.3, May 2024, p.431-432).

Authors: Kazuki Nemoto, Reo Tanaka, and Mari Izumikawa.

The report summarizes discussions from expert talks, panel sessions, and group discussions, including:

- alignment between AI behavior and human values
- implementation challenges in safety, fairness, and transparency
- the need for interdisciplinary approaches beyond engineering, including policy and social design
